Welcome to the definitive version of Pixel Kingdom Adventure! A beautiful, feature-rich retro platformer.



How to Play

Controls

Start Game: Press Space on the title screen.

Move: Arrow Keys or WASD.

Jump: Space, W, or Up Arrow.

Restart: R (on Game Over screen).

At the end of the jungle (around 15,000 pixels), you will find the Arena.



The Boss: The Orc King is huge and wears golden armor.

Attacks: He will chase you and perform a Ground Pound jump. Avoid the shockwave!

Strategy: Jump on his head 5 times to defeat him.

Reward: Defeating him clears the level and wins the game!
